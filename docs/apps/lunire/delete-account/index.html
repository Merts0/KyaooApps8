Lunire Account Deletion Request

Thank you for using the Lunire application. To request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

Data We Collect and Store

Lunire stores certain user-related information on our secure server to enable cross-device synchronization and account management. This data includes:

  • Account credentials (e.g., **email address** used for registration).
  • **Book data** (titles, pages, and related notes/markings).
  • **Uploaded images/photos** associated with your books.

How to Submit Your Deletion Request

Since we handle data deletion manually to ensure all associated records are fully removed from our servers, please send your request to the following dedicated email address:

Our Deletion Email: 8kyaoo@gmail.com

Please ensure your email includes the following:

  • Subject Line: "Permanent Account Deletion Request - Lunire"
  • The email address or username you used to register your Lunire account.

Deletion Timeframe

Upon receiving your request, we will process the permanent deletion of your account and all stored data (email, book information, and associated images) within a maximum of 30 days.

Note: Deleting your account will result in the permanent loss of all your saved books and data.
