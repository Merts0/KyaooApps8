Privacy Policy for Nouva

Last updated: March 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Nouva ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Nouva mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Profile data: name, age, gender, height, weight, dietary goal, activity level, dietary restrictions, and personal struggles you choose to share
  • Food logs: meal names, calorie and macronutrient values, meal type, and time of logging
  • Food photos: images you take or upload for nutritional analysis (processed and not permanently stored on our servers)
  • Chat messages: messages you send to the AI nutrition coach

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Anonymous user identifier: a randomly generated ID assigned at first launch to associate your data with your account without requiring registration
  • Usage data: daily photo analysis count, to enforce free tier limits

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide core features: calculate personalized calorie and macronutrient targets based on your profile
  • Analyze food photos: send your food images to our AI service (Anthropic Claude) to identify foods and estimate nutritional values
  • Power the AI coach: send relevant context (your profile, today's meals, nutrition goals) to generate personalized responses
  • Generate weekly reports: produce your weekly nutrition analysis using aggregated meal data
  • Sync your data: back up your meal logs and profile to our servers so your data persists across sessions
  • Improve the App: use aggregated, anonymized usage patterns to fix bugs and improve features

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

Supabase

We use Supabase as our backend infrastructure for authentication, database storage, and serverless functions. Your profile data and meal logs are stored on Supabase servers located in the EU (Frankfurt, Germany). Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Anthropic (Claude AI)

When you use food photo analysis, the AI coach, or weekly analysis features, relevant data (food images, profile context, meal summaries) is sent to https://anthropic.com for processing by the Claude AI model. Anthropic does not use this data to train its models by default. Privacy Policy: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

Google Play Billing

If you subscribe to Nouva Premium, payment is processed entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment card information. Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Camera and Photo Access

The App requests camera permission to allow you to take food photos for nutritional analysis. Photos are:

  • Compressed on your device before transmission
  • Sent to our AI service solely to identify food and estimate nutritional values
  • Not stored on our servers — only the resulting data (food name, calories, macros) is saved to your account

5. Data Storage and Security

  • On-device: Meal logs, profile, and chat history are stored locally using an encrypted SQLite database
  • Cloud: Data is synced to Supabase servers encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest
  • Retention: Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile screen

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: your profile and meal history are visible within the App
  • Edit your data: update your profile from the Profile screen at any time
  • Delete your data: delete your account via Profile → Account → Delete Account
  • Opt out of AI features: use manual food entry at any time without submitting photos or using the AI coach

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the GDPR, including the right to data portability and to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

7. Children's Privacy

Nouva is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Anonymous Accounts

Nouva does not require an email or password. We assign you an anonymous identifier at first launch. If you uninstall the App without deleting your account first, your anonymous data may remain on our servers until you request deletion via email.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by displaying a notice within the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
